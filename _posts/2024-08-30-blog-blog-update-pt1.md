# August 2024 Blog Update: The Path Forward

### TL;DR

Some major changes will be coming to this blog over the coming weeks and months. The scope of the posts will be expanded to include tutorials, general software/hardware tips, and philosophy. The blog will also receive an accessibility overhaul, including captioned images and animations, translations for multiple languages, and short summaries like the one you're reading now. Finally, content will be become more frequent because of a new two week release schedule.

## Introduction

When I first had the idea to start this blog in November of 2023, I originally intended for it to serve as a public place for me to write about my engineering projects and hobbies. I love what I've written so far, especially [my last post](https://wsfhahn.github.io/2024/02/12/blog-fresh-set-of-eyes.html), which I spent the most time on out of any post on this blog. However, since I last posted in February, I've grown a lot both professionally and personally. In order to both set the bar higher for myself and transform this blog into something I'm proud to share, I'll be making some major changes in the form of many incremental updates.

## Broader Scope

Moving forward, this blog will feature more than just my engineering project reports. I'm excited to announce that I'll be introducing several new genres and formats to my blog to provide a much more diverse reading experience.

### Tutorials

Firstly, I'll be adding a dedicated section for tutorials for those who want to follow along with what I'm doing and join me in building something great. These posts will be broken down into digestible steps that anyone with the right attitude can follow along. They will also be written in non-technical language to make them accessible to hobbyists of all levels of experience.

Most excitingly, I'll be including "starter kit" downloads in my tutorials for a quick and easy setup. Each starter kit will be an archive of all of the files I used in its respective tutorial.

### Code Snippets

One of my favorite additions I'll be making to the blog is a section called Code Snippets. Every time I discover something new or handy on my computer, I'll jot it down and queue it up for posting. It'll contain everything from shell scripts to websites I find useful.

These posts will be much less heavily edited and much, much shorter. They will contain some basic information about why I found something useful, but may require a deeper understanding of the topic to make use of.

### Philosophy

The last new section for this blog I currently have planned is a philosophy section. Reserving time for myself to sit down and think has always been something that's very important to me, and sometimes I think of something that I really want to share. The Philosophy section will serve as a place for me to share anything philosophical I happen to find particularly interesting.

The posts I make in this new section will be both comments about existing philosophical texts and entirely original content. I intend to cite all works I reference within the posts in this section.

## Expanded Accessibility

As I've grown as a person, ensuring that the things I create are accessible to everyone has become very important to me. At my last job, I learned a lot about what it means for something to be truly accessible to all. I believe everyone benefits from a proactive approach to accessibility, so, I'll be taking some steps to ensure there are no barriers between my content and anyone with a working internet connection.

### Captioning

From this point on, all images included in posts on this blog will be captioned. Some captions may be computer written, but they'll all be manually reviewed to ensure that they're correct and descriptive.

Some posts, especially tutorials, may require embedded videos or animations. Any animated content will be captioned fully, with either subtitles or an expandable section within the text.

### Multiple Languages

Thanks to recent advancements in machine learning and software, translation has become easy, practical, and nearly free, even for complex concepts and ideas. Because of this, there's no reason my blog shouldn't be readable for people who don't understand English.

Over the coming weeks, as I overhaul my blog, I'm going to add an option to switch the language of the content to any of the following languages:

* English
* Spanish
* French
* Portuguese

In the future, as I develop a robust workflow for translating my posts, I'll add more languages to continue to expand the accessibility of my blog.

### Too Long; Didn't Read

From now on, every new post above 100 words will have a quick summary in the header. With this change, I'm aiming to allow readers to make quick, informed decisions about whether the content contains what they're looking for.

## Release Schedule

The final major change I have planned for this blog involves the release schedule. I have a lot of ideas written down which I'm so excited to explore and write about. Although I haven't created a concrete release schedule for each genre, I will be aiming to publish one post every two weeks, at the minimum. Content may be released more frequently, but the longest regular delay between two posts will be two weeks.

## To Those who Made it this Far

Thanks for reading about the changes I have planned for my blog. I hope you're as excited as I am for what I have planned! Stay tuned, as the two week release schedule starts now.
