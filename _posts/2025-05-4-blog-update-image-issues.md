## The Path Forward

Hello dear reader, and happy Summer! I, for one, am excited to have sunny days, time with family, and an open schedule. As some have noticed, my regular posts to this blog have slowed, and less progress is being made. But fear not! This blog is not dead. With plenty of open time on the horizon, I will be resuming my work on this blog and continuing to write cool engineering/general content.

## What's Been Going On?

Despite the blog appearing largely untouched, a few things have happened since my last post. First, the whole site stopped working recently, and I needed to fix it. The site was originally based off of a template I found on GitHub, which was causing all sorts of issues. Working on the blog was difficult, and I couldn't easily pick up working on the blog on a new computer, because it required downgrading Ruby (I am not very familiar with Ruby as a whole). So, I took the time to get familiar with Jekyll, and I wrote a new site and transferred all of my content to it (which you're looking at now). It's a breath of fresh air to have a site that's super simple to set up and make changes to.

I'm also aware of an issue where some images are not correctly rendering, especially on mobile. I'm in the process of fixing this issue, and the images in most posts are back up and working. This was because I was pointing to the images incorrectly in my posts, causing me to get rate limited by GitHub. As always, I appreciate feedback from friends and readers who make me aware of these issues. The dedicated Jekyll community has also been very helpful in resolving these issues.
